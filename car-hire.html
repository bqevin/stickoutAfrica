Car Hire

We offer chauffer driven and self drive car hire service affordable rates. For NGOs, Researchers, Corporate bodies, Expatriates, Executives, Church organizations, Embassies and other range of organizations we offer long term hire/rental services at very competitive monthly rate, Unlimited mileage coverage at low prices.

Automatic or manual transmission vehicles are available. We deliver vehicles at client’s door step or airport on arrival. For group travel/conference transportation and airport transfers, we can provide 25 seater shuttle buses . Taxi services for town run are also promptly available.
